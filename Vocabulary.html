Some books that can help you improve your English

“McGraw-Hill Essential ESL Dictionary”

The first thing you’re going to need is a good dictionary so you can look up those interesting words you come across and this book is perfect for it.

"English Vocabulary in Use Series"

The English Vocabulary in Use series is a best-selling series of books by Cambridge University Press that help English students of all levels improve their vocabulary and work towards mastering the English language.

“Oxford Picture Dictionary”

If you’re a visual learner like me, you can really appreciate a dictionary that relies on beautiful photos and illustrations to teach vocabulary.The Oxford Picture Dictionary” is perfect for this.
